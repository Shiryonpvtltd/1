What We’re Building

SHIRYON is not another activewear label. We are creating a new category: Dedicated Gymwear—apparel engineered for squats, pulls, and presses. The global fitness apparel market (~$200 billion by 2028) is saturated—but strength-focused athletes remain overlooked.

While yoga, running, and lifestyle segments dominate, the weight room has been left behind. SHIRYON fills that gap.
